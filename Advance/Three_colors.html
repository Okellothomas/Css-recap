Types of colors

There are various types of colors that can be accessed in the vscode

  1. by name: eg red, blue, yellow
  2. hexadecimal: #000000-black, #ffffff-white
  3. rgb(red, green black): rgb(0, 0, 0)
  4. rgba(red, green, black, opacity): the opacity ranges from 0 to 1 with 0 denoting most opacity and 1 denoting no opacity at all.
  5. hsl(huge, saturation, lightness)
